Mahatma Gandhi

The Father of the Indian Nation

Key Moments in Gandhi's Life

  • 1869 - Born in Porbandar, Gujarat, India
  • 1888 - Traveled to London to study law
  • 1893 - Went to South Africa to practice law, where he developed his philosophy of nonviolent resistance
  • 1915 - Returned to India and joined the Indian National Congress
  • 1919 - Launched the Non-Cooperation Movement against British rule
  • 1930 - Led the famous Salt March to protest the British salt tax
  • 1942 - Launched the Quit India Movement demanding immediate independence
  • 1947 - India gained independence from British rule
  • 1948 - Assassinated by Nathuram Godse in New Delhi
